Web App · SaaS · Product Management Tool
Prioritize
The all-in-one workspace for PMs and Founders to rank features, squash bugs, and align teams on the roadmap.
RICE Framework Team Collaboration Roadmapping Web App B2B SaaS Dark UI

The Problem

Product teams waste countless hours in debates about what to build next — driven by the loudest voice in the room rather than data. Backlogs become graveyards, and roadmaps drift from user value.

The Solution

Prioritize brings structured, data-driven decision-making to any product team. It automates feature scoring using the RICE method, democratizes prioritization through team voting, and visualizes strategy on a live roadmap.

PM Thinking

Applied the RICE scoring model (Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort) to remove subjectivity from prioritization. Built team-voting mechanics to surface ground-level insights — ensuring decisions are collaborative, not top-down. Roadmap is drag-and-drop to support agile re-planning.

Key Features

  • Smart Scoring — RICE auto-calculation with customizable weights
  • Team Voting — democratic prioritization across departments
  • Roadmap View — drag-and-drop timeline visualization
  • Priority Calculator — interactive demo for onboarding
  • Multi-project Dashboard — manage up to 10+ active projects
  • Item Types — Stories, Bugs, Features with due dates

Web App · AI Tool · Generative AI
RAG Document Chatbot
Upload any document and ask questions in plain English — answers grounded in your files, not hallucinations.
RAG LLM Generative AI Document Q&A Web App AI Agent

The Problem

Knowledge workers spend 30%+ of their day searching through documents, PDFs, and reports to find answers buried in long files. Traditional search is keyword-based and misses context entirely.

The Solution

A Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) powered chatbot that ingests your documents and lets you ask any question conversationally. Answers are sourced directly from your files — with citations shown for every response.

PM Thinking

Identified document-search as a high-friction, daily pain point and built the MVP around a single core use case: ask a question, get a grounded answer. Added "Agent Mode" as a power-user feature for complex multi-step queries. Source citations build trust and reduce hallucination risk.

Key Features

  • Multi-document upload — PDF, DOCX, TXT up to 20MB
  • Conversational Q&A — natural language answers with citations
  • Agent Mode — fast single-pass retrieval for direct facts
  • Search within document — filter answers to a specific file
  • Clear chat history — session management for privacy
  • Deploy-ready — one-click deployment interface

Mobile App · Android · Consumer
Habit Tracker
A habit-building app with daily check-ins, weekly insights, streaks, and a gamified achievement system that keeps users coming back.
Gamification Engagement Loops Behavioral Design Mobile App Android Consumer

The Problem

Most habit apps are either cluttered with features or too simple to be useful. Users start strong then drop off because there's no meaningful feedback loop — no sense of progress or reward for consistency.

The Solution

A mobile-first habit tracker focused on three things: a clean daily view, meaningful weekly insights, and a gamified achievement system that rewards long-term consistency — not just one-day wins.

PM Thinking

Designed around the Hook Model — cue (daily notification), routine (check-in), reward (streak + achievement unlocked). Weekly Insights convert raw data into personalized coaching ("Tuesday is your best day"). 17 achievements create progressive mastery and long-term retention.

Key Features

  • Today View — daily progress ring with all habits listed
  • Weekly calendar — visual streak tracking across 7 days
  • Stats & Weekly Insights — completion rate, perfect days, best/worst day
  • Achievements system — 17 badges unlocked progressively
  • Habit customization — emoji, notes, skip, reorder
  • Profile — check-ins, best streak, perfect days at a glance
